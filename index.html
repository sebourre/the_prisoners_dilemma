Rules

The game is quite simple.
You and an another prisoner will face each other during a limited number of rounds.
You both have two choices, either cooperate or defect.

If both of you cooperate, you and your opponent will gain 3 points.
If you cooperate and your opponent defects, you won't gain any point but your opponent will gain 5 points.
If you defect and your opponent cooperates, you will gain 5 points and your opponent won't gain any point.
If both of you defect, you and your opponent will gain 1 point.

The goal is to finish the game with more points than your opponents.
